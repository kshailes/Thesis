Police chief visits banks, ATMs 
 Commissioner of Police D. Gautam Sawang on Thursday visited several banks and ATMs in the city and interacted with people who were waiting to withdraw cash.In a bid to learn and address issues being faced by the customers directly, Mr. Sawang visited SBH in Patamata, SBI in One-Town and a few others in city. He spoke to bank employees as well as women and senior citizens.
