High Court grants bail to Jayachandra

The High Court of Karnataka on Tuesday granted bail to S.C. Jayachandra, suspended chief project officer of the State Highway Development Project, in a money laundering case booked against him by the Enforcement Directorate (ED) after he was booked by the Central Bureau of Investigation for allegedly indulging in illegal exchange of demonetised currencies.Justice Rathnakala passed the order while allowing a petition filed by him seeking release on bail. He was arrested on December 3, 2016.The ED has alleged that Jayachandra misused his official position and used three other accused persons to launder his ill-gotten money and used it to acquire properties through “proceeds of crime”.
