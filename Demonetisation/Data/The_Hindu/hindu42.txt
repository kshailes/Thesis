Demonetisation: a year later

The Hindu reported extensively on the effects of the move last year. Nearly everyone faced inconvenience; businesses, especially small enterprises that dealt in cash, took big hits. For many of those seriously affected, life was further complicated by the introduction of the Goods and Services Tax. A year after the initial announcement, we take a look at how the two initiatives have throttled business. Neha Nalawade reports.
