12 held for exchanging old currency

 The police on Sunday took a 12-member gang into custody when it reportedly tried to exchange banned big notes with new currency.On a tip-off that some persons were engaged in exchanging of currency in One Town area, police rounded up the gang and seized about Rs. 18 lakh, including some new Rs. 2,000 notes, from them.The police are trying to find out how and from which bank the gang had drawn the amount.
